Sale&Safe

Sale&Safe, is a web application created in this case
for an appliance company called Tech, where the catalog of
is shown your products, by category. Here the client can log in, to be able to perform
your order, which will be stored in a shopping cart, where once it is filled with all
the products you want, you can indicate the quantity, see the prices, the total price, for
finally make or cancel the purchase.
